Analytical Methodology

Seismic AI utilizes a rule-based deterministic engine to estimate structural vulnerability. Our approach focuses on three core pillars of seismic engineering:

  • Soil-Structure Interaction (SSI): Soft soils like Clay can amplify seismic waves by up to 1.6x compared to bedrock, directly impacting structural response.
  • Structural Resonance: We calculate the interplay between building height (floors) and wave frequency to predict shear stress.
  • Material Ductility: Steel frames offer superior energy dissipation compared to traditional brick masonry.
